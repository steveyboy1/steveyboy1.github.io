My responsibilities at Acrosmith Gymnastics consist of teaching the Ninja Class for the six to thirteen-year-olds and assisting in coaching a junior tunbling class. The Ninja Class focuses on fundemental movement skills such as climbing, swinging, and crawling, and also gives an introduction to beginner Parkour and gymnastics skills. The tumbling class ranges from six to fifteen-year-olds and focuses on imporving tumbling skills and helping kids get started in tumbling.
